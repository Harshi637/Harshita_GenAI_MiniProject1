# Prompt Library for Content Generation
## Mini Project – Generative AI using RCTF Framework

**Framework Used:** RCTF  
- **R** → Role  
- **C** → Context  
- **T** → Task  
- **F** → Format

# Prompt 9: FAQ Generator

## Purpose
Generate frequently asked questions for products or services.

## Prompt
I am a customer support specialist responsible for preparing FAQ sections for a company website. The goal is to help users quickly understand common concerns related to products or services. Your task is to generate five frequently asked questions and answers for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. The questions should address common customer doubts, and the answers should be simple, clear, and concise. Format the response as question-and-answer pairs.

## Output(PRODUCT-shampoo)
1. Q: Is this shampoo suitable for all hair types?

**A:** Yes, the shampoo is designed to work effectively on most hair types, including dry, oily, and normal hair.

2. Q: Can I use this shampoo every day?

**A:** Yes, the gentle formula is safe for regular daily use without causing excessive dryness.

3. Q: Does the shampoo help reduce hair fall?

**A:** The shampoo helps strengthen hair and reduce breakage, which may help minimize hair fall caused by weak or damaged hair.

4. Q: Is the shampoo safe for colored or treated hair?

**A:** Yes, the formula is mild and suitable for colored or chemically treated hair.

5. Q: How long does it take to see visible results?

**A:** Many users notice softer and healthier-looking hair after a few washes, while long-term improvements may take a few weeks of regular use.
